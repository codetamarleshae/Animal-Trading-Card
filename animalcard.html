Hedgehog

Immune to many poisonous plants, the hedgehog will eat them and spread their poisonous saliva all over their spikes for protection against predators!

  • Scientific Name: Erinaceinae
  • Average Length: 8-10 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 2-5 years
  • Habitat: Woodlands, parks and gardens

While they're known for their ability to roll into a tight ball in defense of predators, this nocturnal 1 to 2lb mammal is also lactose intolerant. However, their taste for insects can make them quite the little helper for pests in gardens.
